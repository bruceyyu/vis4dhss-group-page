About Us

Similar to other data formats such as text and images, visualizations are increasingly created, stored, shared, and (re-)used with AI techniques. In vision of formalizing visualizations as an emerging data format, we apply addvanced AI techniques to visualization data (AI4VIS). And we have explored a diverse topic of chart configuration, computer-aided inforgraphics, and graph layout.
